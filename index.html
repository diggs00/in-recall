Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Why is my Chevrolet Spark/ Beat/ Enjoy being recalled?

    A: At Chevrolet Sales India Pvt. Ltd (Chevrolet), we give utmost priority to product quality and customer safety & satisfaction. During our ongoing internal evaluation processes and routine quality checks, we’ve identified a need to inspect the GM approved Remote Keyless Entry Installation system (RKE System) in certain Chevrolet vehicles manufactured between 2007 - 2014 (For Enjoy 2013 & 2014 only) and repair the installation, if required on free of charge basis.

  • Q: Why is this Campaign conducted?

    A: GM India has been made aware of a situation where a RKE System was installed incorrectly on 2007-2014 Chevrolet Spark, Beat and Enjoy models. This in some very isolated cases can prompt sparks resulting in potential damage.

  • Q: Is it safe to drive my Chevrolet Spark/Beat/Enjoy?

    A: Yes. Safety is our first priority. The Chevrolet Spark, Beat and Enjoy RKE System meets all the relevant regulatory standards but if the engine gets warm and the indicator light turns on, drivers should pull over and allow the engine to cool to restore normalcy).

  • Q: Will these repairs affect the performance of my Chevrolet Vehicle?

    A: Not at all. The repairs shall have no effect on the vehicle performance.

  • Q: When will I get my Spark/Beat/Enjoy back post repairs? Will you collect it? Who will deliver it back?

    A: The inspection and recommended repairs shall take approximately 1 hours’ time. Therefore, the customers are requested to take their vehicles to their nearest Chevrolet dealership for inspection and repair free of charge with a prior appointment.  A service support team (comprising of Dealer Service Manger & Dealer CRM) has been put in place. They will also contact customers to schedule an appointment for the required repairs.

  • Q: What all will you repair in the Vehicle / kind of repairs that would be carried out?

    A: Chevrolet has developed a solution after carrying out required engineering validations. We shall inspect the GM approved Remote Keyless Entry (RKE) Installation system in Chevrolet vehicles and repair the RKE installation, if required.

  • Q: What will be the expense?

    A: It would be carried out free of charge by our local Chevrolet Authorized dealer.

  • Q: My Chevrolet Spark/Beat/Enjoy has been giving certain other problems. Is it due to the same issue? Will I be compensated?

    A: No, this issue is specific to the RKE System and has no bearing on the other concerns reported by you. In this campaign, only installation of RKE system will be repaired free of charge, if required. If your car is presenting some problems, you should simply stop by one of our authorized dealers and get it repaired. Our teams are dedicated to serving our customers efficiently and quickly.

  • Q: I am/ my friend is planning to buy a Chevrolet Spark/Beat/Enjoy, will this problem be fixed before purchase?

    A: Yes. You can go ahead with your purchase. This recall has no bearing on 2015 onwards vehicles.

  • Q: My vehicle is fitted with Remote Keyless Entry system from after market, will this also be covered under the recall?

    A: Chevrolet always insists on usage of GM approved accessories so that the customers are provided with such after sales services. Chevrolet neither recommends usage of any non GM approved outside fitment in the vehicles nor does it approve tampering of electrical connections. If your Vehicle is fitted with non GM approved RKE system fitted from after market, it is not covered under this recall scheme. Note: For these set of customer's dealership to share customer & Vehicle details with GMI over mail @ gmi.cac@gm.com separately.

  • Q: I have been sold a wrong product. I should be either given a replacement or compensation for the damages.

    A: This issue is specific to the Remote Keyless Entry system and has no bearing on the other parts of the vehicle. Your Chevrolet is a good and safe car. As safety is our No.1 concern, we will repair the RKE System free of charge.
